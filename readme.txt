Customer Experience Analytics for Growth Hacking. Supports WooCommerce 2.x.

== Description ==

= Perfect for Marketing and Sales Acceleration =

Group people by their characteristics and behavior patterns. New customers, returning visitors, subscribers, etc may be captured. Research each audience's behavior, conversion rates, average price to better interact with it and drive purchases.

= Optimize Customer Journeys =

Analyze your clients' online and offline experiences to find meaningful patterns and opportunities for improvement. Refine customer journeys or design new ones to capture and convert more leads, enhance LTV.

= Boost Sales with Experiments =

Perform experiments on how to improve the quality of customer interactions, raise conversions, and maximize repeat buying. Automatically track the results and store them to monitor how your hypotheses are getting more valuable.

== Installation ==

1. Install plugin from WordPress Plugin Directory or upload `cxmap` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory manually.
2. Activate plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress admin panel.
